1. In a large pot, cover potatoes with cold water. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to a simmer.
2. Cook until potatoes are tender and offer no resistance when poked with a paring knife. Drain and let stand until cool enough to handle.
3. Peel the potatoes
4. Pass potatoes through ricer
5. Add butter, cream, and salt to potatoes over low heat
6. Fold in butter and cream until incorporated
